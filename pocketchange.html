PocketChange Mobile App

Project Summary

PocketChange redefines what it means to save money quickly and efficiently without all of the confusion on conventual finance apps. PocketChange is a modern and easy to use personal finance app that helps you save money with ease. Open an account, input income and expenses, track spending, visual finances, and save money.

My Role

UI/UX Designer

Duration

1 Month

Overview

Who is the app for?

This app is for people who want to save money, quickly, for any particular reason. The users are not new to technology, but do not like working with their finances. So, they require that the information they receive via the app is accessible and clear.

What kind of tasks will the user complete while they use this app?

This mobile app displays all data on income and expenses which can be recorded easily and on the go. Users will be able to open an account, input income and expenses, track spending, visual finances, create goals, securely add personal financial information in formation, and save money.

Why would someone want to use this app?

Saving money can be really hard, especially when you don't have a long time to do so. By providing personalized information on how a user can save based on their actual finances, the app itself can provide financial advice during these finite periods of saving.

Competitor Analysis

I researched and studied many popular finance apps on the market. The outcome was the creation of my idea to create a clean and modern app that would fit in with well known and trusted apps on the app store. The app would be an easy to use and an empowering money saving tool that to any person, even with no financial experience, could use.
