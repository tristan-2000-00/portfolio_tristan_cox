Overview

Who is the App for?

This app is for people who want to save money, quickly, for any particular reason. The users are not new to technology, but do not like working with their finances. So, they require that the information they receive via the app is accessible and clear.

What kind of tasks can the user perform with the app?

This mobile app displays all data on income and expenses which can be recorded easily and on the go. Users will be able to open an account, input income and expenses, track spending, visual finances, create goals, securely add personal financial information in formation, and save money.

Why would someone want to use this app?

Saving money can be really hard, especially when you don't have a long time to do so. By providing personalized information on how a user can save based on their actual finances, the app itself can provide financial advice during these finite periods of saving.
