About

My Story

Hi, I'm a UI/UX designer currently based in Missoula, Montana. I started my design journey at the University of Vermont, graduating with a degree in art and computer science. I have a passion for visual design and creating meaningful products that bring people together. I am data and research driven to produce clean and logical interfaces.

When I'm not at my desk, you can find me in the mountains. I explore nature year round, no matter the weather, whether that's hiking, skiing, or biking. I love to capture nature through photography and painting which I use as design inspiration.
